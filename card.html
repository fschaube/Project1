Chameleon

Chameleons are able to change their skin colouration.
That makes them a master of disguise.

  • Scientific Name: Chamaeleonidae
  • Habitats: Rain forest, desert
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Average Length: 30 centimeters

The chameleon has an average length of 30 centimeters. You can find it especially in the tropical rain forest. A chameleon tongue can accelerate from
0 to 60 mph in 1/100th of a second.
